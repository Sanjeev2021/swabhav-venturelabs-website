Assess Your Current and Desired State

To ensure a successful product transformation, it’s essential to assess where you are and determine what you want to achieve.

Identify the "Why"
  • Define your organization's mission, strategic goals, go-to-market strategy & key metrics.
  • Once you have a clear 'why,' adopt a product mindset for your transformation.
  • Assess your organization's maturity to evaluate your current state & pinpoint where to being.
Next steps to progress your journey
  • Align stakeholders & obtain consensus over "the why" & a mutual knowledge of the obstacles holding you back.
  • Use your existing reality as a guide, define your vision & set a priority list of areas to concentrate on, like upskilling your product teams or change management in team(s).
  • A smart place to start is by measuring outcome-oriented product success metrics, including feature utilization.
  • To help lead and steer the shift, find people who have already made the adjustment and who can serve as evangelists and coaches.
